The omelet recipe

:p

ingredients

  • 4 tsp. unsalted butter, divided

Directions

  1. Melt 2 teaspoons of the butter in an 10-inch nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add the red and green bell pepper and onions. Cook, stirring occasionally, until softened, 6 to 8 minutes. Add the ham, scallion, 1/8 teaspoon of the salt and a pinch of black pepper. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the scallion is tender, about 1 minute. Transfer the mixture to a plate; set aside. Wipe the skillet clean.
